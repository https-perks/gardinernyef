Mental Health Initiative

Supporting student well-being through partnership, prevention, and community care.

North Yellowstone Education Foundation seeks to meet the mental and behavioral health needs of our community through partnership, targeted support, and school-connected programming.

We believe students and families thrive when they have access to practical mental health resources, caring professionals, and strong local systems of support.

Our Partnership with Rural Resilience

NYEF supports mental and behavioral health in our community through partnership with Rural Resilience.

Rural Resilience connects rural communities to behavioral and mental health solutions while protecting social well-being and fostering resilience. Their work helps strengthen support systems for students, families, and communities across rural Park County.

NYEF has supported this work through partnership, grant support, and ongoing collaboration to help expand access to services in Gardiner and surrounding communities.
