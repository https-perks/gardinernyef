Opportunity to Serve

NYEF Seeking Grant Selection Team Members

Help support classroom innovation by serving on the Connect! Grant Selection Team.

Would you be interested in assisting with the Connect! Grant Selection Team?

Connect! Grants are our classroom grants program. They support projects that fill educational gaps, promote health and wellness, and reach a wide range of students.

These grants are community-driven, community-funded, and community-evaluated — meaning funded projects are chosen by a committee of community members.

Complete the signup form below and check the Grant Selection Team option. We will follow up with more information about dates, time commitments, and expectations.
